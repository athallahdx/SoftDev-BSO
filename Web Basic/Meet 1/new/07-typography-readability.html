🎯 Visual Hierarchy

Gunakan ukuran, weight, dan color untuk membuat hierarchy yang jelas: H1 > H2 > H3 > body text.

Main Headline (H1)

Subsection (H3)

💡 Hierarchy Best Practices:

  • Gunakan hanya satu H1 per halaman (untuk judul utama)
  • Urutkan heading dari H1 → H2 → H3 (jangan skip level)
  • Contrast ukuran yang jelas membantu user scan halaman
  • Font weight lebih tebal untuk hierarchy lebih tinggi
  • Color juga bisa menunjukkan hierarchy

🎯 Practical Typography Example

The Power of Good Typography

Published on April 10, 2024 · By John Doe

Good typography isn't just about making text look pretty. It's about communicating effectively and making content easy to read.

Why Typography Matters

Typography affects how users perceive your content. Poor typography can make even great content hard to read and understand. Good typography, on the other hand, guides the reader through your content smoothly.

Key Principles

  1. Hierarchy: Use size and weight to show importance
  2. Readability: Choose readable fonts and sizes
  3. Consistency: Maintain consistent spacing and sizing
  4. Contrast: Ensure text stands out from background

Best Practices

When designing typography, remember these best practices:

  • Use 16px+ for body text on desktop
  • Keep line height between 1.5–1.8 for readability
  • Limit to 2–3 fonts per design
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility
